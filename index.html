Institute for the Industrialization of Mars

How to build cities (and factories) on mars?

Meta problems (in popperian terms):
  • How do civilizations sustain and progress? (And why do they collapse?)
  • How to play factorio? (in real life)
  • How to get back to the future?
  • How to build a functional institute. And design, maintain and evolve an organizational structure that supports the building of large infrastructure technolgies and megaprojects
  • And yes, how to industrialize mars?
Meta research areas:
  • Energy systems. How to have enough energy on mars? Solar/Nuclear?
  • Key material and resource self sufficiency. Steel, plastics, methane, electronics, water and oxygen.
  • The labour problem and autonomy. Robotics.
  • Biotech, food and geoengineering. On making mars green (and blue)
  • Mars native structures (and architecture)
  • (Compact) Manufacturing on mars with rapid protoyping and full stack production using far fewer people.
Specific projects (for now):
  • Exploring solar energy's impact on energy-intensive industries.
  • Steel smelting. Blast furnace for low gravity and low pressure atmosphere.
  • Reducing the time of automation planning of manufacturing facilities.
